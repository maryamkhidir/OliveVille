A Perfect Place to Nurture your Wards
Olive Ville school is a newly established Creche, Pre-nursery, and Nursery school, founded with the aim of nurturing kids with both Western and Moral education. Our focus on toddlers and preschoolers is borne out of our passion for laying the solid foundation needed to excel.

The Solid Background For the Future
We understand that getting the right basis of knowledge for youngsters can be stressful- this is why we have taken it upon ourselves to help in training them to become the best version of themselves. At Olive Ville Schools, our admission process is very smooth and we are currently taking in new sets of world leaders into our creche, pre-nursery, and nursery classes. Our pupils are accepted from 3 months up to 3 years old so that they can be well groomed and prepared for their next endeavors.
Globally Accepted Curriculum
Our pupils are taught in accordance with the globally accepted curriculum- our Montessori mode of teaching will not only make the learning process fun for your wards, but they will also help them in quick identification and lettering, as the case may be.
Daycare Services
We run Daycare services, After school care which include Swimming and Dancing classes, and also games and sporting activities. We also run Music classes, weekend services, holiday care, and feeding services. With Olive Ville, you always have your kid covered and occupied with us.
Health Facilities
At Olive Ville schools, we consider the health of your kids very important. This is why we have regular check-ups done by a child specialist. We also do not rule out the possibility of emergencies, our well-equipped sickbay will come in handy in those cases.
World Class Learning Experience
Through play-based learning, your child gets to play, have fun, and still learn alphabets and numbers. This method not only helps your child with speech but enhances the essential skill of communication.
